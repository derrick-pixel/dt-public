Anchored against Deel/Remote/Multiplier SEA pricing, not the global EOR enterprise floor.

Two artefacts on one page. Market sizing decomposes the SG micro-business EOR opportunity into TAM (every SG entity with 1–10 employees), SAM (those without dedicated HR and not yet on a payroll SaaS), and SOM (the 3-year wedge Elitez EOR can plausibly capture at S$59/mo). The pricing strategy half profiles four buyer personas, surfaces willingness-to-pay bands derived from competitor anchors and DIY-displacement arithmetic, and recommends three tiers that map to the buyer set. Cross-link to competitor analytics, whitespace, design audit, the printable report, or the parent Elitez EOR site.

Regulatory landscape

Policies

SG employer-of-record regulation spans CPF Act §7+§87, IRAS Section 11 + IR8A AIS, WICA 2019, MOM Work Pass framework (EFMA §11), IRAS GST registration threshold, IMDA PSG, WSG WDG(JR+), PDPA, and the 2025 Workplace Fairness Act + Shared Parental Leave expansion. Each layer is a forced-statutory failure mode for the DIY founder.
