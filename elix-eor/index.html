FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is an Employer of Record (EOR)? ▼
An EOR legally employs your workers on your behalf. Your employees are technically on our payroll (under Elitez Group), but they work exclusively for you and you retain full operational control — same workspace, same reporting lines, same day-to-day. We handle all the legal and statutory obligations: CPF, WICA, IR8A, payslips, SDL, and benefits administration.
Will my employees know they're under a different company? ▼
Yes, legally their employment contract will be with Elitez Group. However, this is standard practice for EOR arrangements. Many employees appreciate the enhanced benefits (better healthcare, lower WICA costs) that come with being part of a larger group. Their day-to-day work, reporting line, and workspace remain entirely with you.
Who is Elitez EOR best for? ▼
Elitez EOR is designed specifically for micro businesses and sole proprietors in Singapore who employ 1-3 local Singaporean employees and have no foreign workers. If you're a small business owner spending hours each month on CPF submissions and payroll admin — instead of growing your business — we're built for you.
How does the CardUp credit card payment work? ▼
Each month, we invoice you for the total employment cost (salary + CPF + WICA + healthcare + SDL + Elitez fee). Instead of paying by bank transfer, you can pay this invoice through CardUp using your credit card at a preferential 1.7% processing fee. If you use a miles-earning credit card (like DBS Altitude or OCBC 90°N), you'll earn KrisFlyer miles on the entire invoice amount. We absorb the CardUp fee for your first 6 months when you sign a 12-month contract.
What's the contract commitment? ▼
We require a minimum 12-month contract. This allows us to offer the 6-month free CardUp fee promotion and ensures stable employment arrangements for your staff. After the initial 12 months, the contract auto-renews on a month-to-month basis with a 1-month notice period.
Can I still manage my employees' work and schedule? ▼
Absolutely. You retain 100% operational control. You set their tasks, schedule, workspace, performance goals — everything. We only handle the administrative and statutory employment obligations. Think of us as your outsourced HR back-office, not a staffing agency.
What about the $25/month healthcare — what does it cover? ▼
The $25/month healthcare package includes hospital & surgery insurance and outpatient clinic visits. This is a group plan rate that's only possible because your employees are pooled with the wider Elitez Group workforce. An equivalent individual plan would typically cost $80-120/month.
How quickly can I get started? ▼
Onboarding typically takes 5 business days. We handle the employment letter transfer, benefits enrollment, CPF account setup, and WICA coverage — all you need to do is provide your employee details and sign the agreement.
