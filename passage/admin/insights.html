Strategic implications

What the sizing means

The five conclusions Agent 3 (pricing) and Agent 4 (whitespace) downstream of this page must respect. Casket purchase is one-event-per-customer, the SAM caps at S$18M, Muslim shroud-burial exclusion is structural, and direct-cremation cohort is the highest-fit growth wedge.

Regulatory landscape

Policies

SG bereavement regulation: NEA Mandai cremation slots, MUIS Muslim shroud-burial directive, MOH / ICA digital death registration, SFDA voluntary code, SCDF / NEA columbarium rules, PDPA bereavement-data handling, GST treatment, and Tripartite + PWM workforce posture. None mandate casket-line pricing transparency — that gap is the wedge.

Pricing strategy

Four personas with structured NBA arithmetic

Each persona's nba.monthly_sgd_equivalent represents the displaced spend for this one-event purchase — the casket line buried inside the traditional parlour bundle (~S$2,500 mid-point) or the alternative DTC option. The expected WTP must sit between 0.4× and 1.2× that anchor for the tier to be defensible.

/ § 04 · n=4 personas
Tier ladder

Recommended pricing tiers

Three tiers map directly to the three published Passage SKUs: Serene S$499 (lead-gen / direct-cremation wedge), Tranquil S$999 (centre-of-bullseye for adult-child anti-pressure), Eternal S$1,299 (pre-arrangement + eco-values upper-stretch). PSG / EDG grants are not applicable to consumer casket purchases — effective price = list price.
