Feature · A case study

Tok Meiting, 45,000 vested options, and fifteen more months to the window.

What does vesting look like in practice? Take one holder's FY2022 grant, halfway through its five-year life, and walk the numbers to the exercise date.

The 60,000-option grant issued to Tok Meiting on 15 July 2022 was the company's largest first-round allocation to a non-founding executive. It vests in two parts: a 20-per-cent cliff at the first anniversary, then 80 per cent over forty-eight monthly tranches. Today — two years, nine months in — exactly 45,000 options have vested, or 75 per cent.
