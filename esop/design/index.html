Four ways to render Elitez ESOP.

Same content, four visual languages. Click into each, scroll, compare the feel. Tell me which one to commit across the real app.

Candidate variants

01

Institutional Report

Serif-dominant, cream paper, orange reserved for the one or two most important numbers. Reads like a private-bank quarterly — the tone our legal document deserves.

02

Modern SaaS

White surfaces, Inter throughout, rounded cards, generous orange on actions and data. The look every fintech startup has converged on — familiar, fast to learn, less weighty.

Open in full
03

Editorial

Oversized Fraunces headlines, full-bleed navy bands, orange drop caps and pull quotes. The platform as a serious publication — confident, distinctive, harder to produce if we ever extend it.

Open in full
04

Terminal

Near-black background, mono numbers, orange data highlights. Bloomberg-energy but modernised. Power-user density for the Committee; the one variant likely to scare non-finance employees.

Open in full
Honest trade-offs 01 and 03 carry the gravitas of a legal/equity instrument. 02 is the easiest to maintain and extend. 04 is striking but alienates the 25 holders who aren't in finance and would see the platform on their phones. My default recommendation: 01 with a few operational pages borrowing 02's density.
