Welcome to your ESOP portal.

Your option grants, your vesting curve, your exercise calendar, your statements — all live in one secure place. This is a short guide to getting in and finding what you need.

1Get into the portal

i.
Open the invitation
Look for an email from noreply@elitez.com.sg. It contains a one-time secure link. (If you don't see it in 10 minutes, check your spam folder.)
ii.
Set your password
Click the link — it opens a page where you choose your own password. Minimum 12 characters. The portal never stores your password in plain text.
iii.
Sign in
Bookmark esop.elitez.com.sg. From now on, sign in with your work email + the password you chose.
If anything feels off
Forgot your password? Use "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page. Suspect the email is phishing? Confirm with HR or Derrick before clicking — the legitimate sender is always noreply@elitez.com.sg.

2What you'll see inside

Portfolio
Your grants by FY, vesting %, value today, and a per-grant exercise calculator. The headline number is "value if exercised now".
Plan Rules
The Plan in plain English — vesting, exercise, FMV, leavers, exit events, trading window — indexed to the binding clauses.
Trading
After your first exercise, you can list to sell during the Jan 16-31 trading window. Until then, this tab shows the calendar.
Your documents
Letter of Offer, Acceptance Form, Exercise Notice, annual statements — all generated as PDFs you can download whenever you need.

5Quick answers

When can I actually sell?
Not until you exercise. Then, only in the next January trading window (16–31 Jan). If you exercise in Jan 2027, you can first list to sell in Jan 2028.
How is FMV decided?
Max of (EBITDA × 6) and Net Tangible Assets, divided by the share base. From the audited FY ending 30 April, published by 30 November.
What's taxed and when?
At exercise, 90% of FMV × shares exercised is Singapore employment income. Future gains are generally capital gains for SG residents — not taxed. Speak to a tax advisor.
What if I leave Elitez?
Good Leaver: vested options retained; unvested forfeited. Bad Leaver: all options forfeited. Determination is by the Committee per Clause 11.
