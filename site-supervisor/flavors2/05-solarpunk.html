Three timelines, one convergence.

The body-shop era ends in 2028. Pure-play supervisor agencies cannot satisfy BCA's three pillars — ISO/IEC 17020 firm systems, a permanent core of 1 RE + 3 RTOs employed for twelve consecutive months, and SAC-calibrated measurement technologies — without a platform behind them.

For Elitez, the marginal cost is roughly half the greenfield equivalent. Five Group assets matter: Adept Academy WSQ school, the ISO 9001 / 27001 / EcoVadis Silver stack, ~2,000 deployed manpower across verticals, the Singapore Polytechnic and ITE Work Study partnerships, and regulatory access via the ACES advisory committee, MOM AfA, and HCP status.
