— Section 02Approach

BCA's 3 September 2025 announcement restructures site supervision around licensed firms. Three pillars: ISO/IEC 17020 firm systems, key personnel including a permanent core of 1 RE plus 3 RTOs employed for at least twelve consecutive months, and SAC-accredited measurement equipment.

For Elitez, the marginal cost is roughly half the greenfield equivalent (S$1.2M+ over 18 months). The platform exists for other reasons — Adept Academy SSG-approved Training Organisation, ISO 9001 / 27001 / EcoVadis Silver, IHRP exclusive partnership, ACES advisory committee seat (which co-administers JAC), and the operating playbook of Elitez Aviation under the Aerospace Labour Supply Scheme.

Two senior Surbana Jurong professionals are joining as co-founders on a 4-year vest with 1-year cliff. The Board has approved S$740K phased equity plus a S$1M revolving working-capital facility, with a hard go/no-go review at Month 12.
