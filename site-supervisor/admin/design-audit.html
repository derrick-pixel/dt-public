Website Design Audit

Every competitor in the run is rated on five design dimensions — visual hierarchy, content density, mobile responsiveness, findability under three buyer intents, and editorial trust signals — then aggregated to a single weighted 1–10 score. Any sub-dimension scoring six or below is required to carry a written reviewer note explaining what failed and what the fix would look like.
