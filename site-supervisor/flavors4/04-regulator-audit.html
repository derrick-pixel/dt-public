Singapore's licensed site supervision firm — built before the 2028 deadline.

On 3 September 2025, the Building & Construction Authority announced firm-based licensing for site supervision under the Building Control Act 1989. From 2028, all building projects above S$75M must engage a BCA-licensed Site Supervision Firm. Elitez Site Supervisor is being incorporated to file SAC accreditation in 2026 and BCA licence by Q1–Q2 2028 — six months ahead of mandatory.
