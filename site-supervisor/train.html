Curriculum tracks

Three pathways into the supervisor pipeline.

TRACK 01 / Reskill

Mid-career conversion

M&E technicians, draftsmen, foremen reskilled into RTO competence. SCTP / CCP funded. 50–90% government salary support during training. Underpinned by the SP MOU and ITE Work Study Chair.

→ Year-3 hire-channel share: 15%

TRACK 02 / BCSS-aligned

Pre-employment conversion

Building Construction Supervisor Safety Course conversion programmes for new RTOs. Direct preparation for JAC accreditation. Bench-training during JAC processing window (typical 6–10 weeks).

→ Reduces JAC-pending bench cost

TRACK 03 / CPD specialist

JAC-recognised CPD

Continuing Professional Development modules for JAC STU credit. Targeted upskilling: PPVC supervision, post-tensioning, geotechnical instrumentation, structural steel. Subject to JAC course approval.

→ Earns STU through internal training

Pipeline

Train → Deploy → Retain.

Adept Academy graduates become brand-loyal deployment candidates. The training relationship is the recruitment relationship.

STAGE 01

Pre-screening

Verify diploma/degree, work experience documentation, BCSS course completion (for new RTOs).

STAGE 02

JAC application

Sponsor and pay application fee; project-work assignment after accreditation. Processing 6–10 weeks.

STAGE 03

Bench training

Internal SOP training, ISO/IEC 17020 awareness, shadow live deployments while JAC accreditation pending.

STAGE 04

Deployment

Initially paired with senior. 90-day performance review. Ongoing CPD via internal STU-eligible courses.
