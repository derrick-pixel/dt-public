02 · The inflection

A twelve to eighteen month head-start window.

Three independent timelines converge between now and 2028. Firms that file for SAC accreditation early hold a defensible competitive position. Pure body shops lose access to the largest segment of the market — projects above S$75M, which from 2028 onward must engage a BCA-licensed Site Supervision Firm.

For Elitez, the marginal cost is roughly half the greenfield equivalent (S$1.2M+ over 18 months), and most of it is consulting time, not capability build. Five Group assets matter — Adept Academy, the ISO 9001 / 27001 / EcoVadis Silver stack, the recruitment platform with 6,228 historical placements, the Singapore Polytechnic and ITE Work Study partnerships, and regulatory access via the ACES advisory committee, MOM Alliance for Action, and Human Capital Partnership status. None replicable on a relevant timeline.
