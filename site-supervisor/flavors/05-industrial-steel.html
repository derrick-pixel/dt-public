THE INFLECTION.

Three independent timelines converge between now and 2028. Macro: Singapore construction at a structural high (S$47–53B 2026 forecast). Regulatory: BCA's firm-based regime closes the largest segment to body shops. Platform: Elitez Group already owns most of the inputs — Adept Academy, ISO stack, ACES committee seat, Aviation/ALSS precedent.

The greenfield equivalent build cost for a Tier-3 challenger is over S$1.2M plus 18 months. For Elitez, the marginal cost is roughly half that, and most of it is consulting time, not capability build. Two senior Surbana Jurong operators are joining as co-founders on a 4-year vest with 1-year cliff. Funding envelope: S$700K equity + S$1M working capital.
