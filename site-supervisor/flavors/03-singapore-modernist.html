A twelve-to-eighteen month head-start window.

Three independent timelines converge between now and 2028. Firms that file SAC accreditation early hold a defensible competitive position. Pure body shops lose access to the largest segment of the market. The risk is not whether the opportunity exists — it is execution velocity. That risk is meaningfully reduced when the venture sits inside Elitez Group rather than as a startup.
