Singapore's licensed site supervision firm — built before the 2028 deadline.

On 3 September 2025, BCA announced that all building projects above S$75M must engage a BCA-licensed Site Supervision Firm from 2028. The body-shop era ends. Elitez Site Supervisor is being incorporated to file for SAC accreditation in 2026 and BCA licence by Q1–Q2 2028 — under the platform of Elitez Group, with Adept Academy as the WSQ-funded training engine.
