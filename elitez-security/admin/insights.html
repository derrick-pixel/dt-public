The pricing strategy half profiles the buyer personas grounded in deck-named clients (MCST committee chairs, property managers, MNC plant EHS directors, religious-site trustees, eldercare operators), surfaces willingness-to-pay bands derived from competitor rate cards + grant arithmetic, and recommends three tiers that map to the buyer set.
