White Space Atlas

Three artefacts work together to identify where the SG security category is open and where Elitez can win. The strategy-canvas radar plots the mapped competitor universe (top-tier APO, mid-tier guarding, tech-only entrants, IFM bundlers) against the dimensions buyers actually evaluate; flat patches signal differentiation opportunities. The segment×need heatmap counts how many competitors genuinely serve each pair (score ≥ 3) — green is whitespace, amber is contested, red is crowded; click any cell for the player list. The ranked attack plans translate findings into concrete go-to-market specs (ICP, TAM, gap thesis, why-we-win, channel, pitch, pricing, content).
