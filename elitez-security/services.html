Our Core Services

Complete Security Solutions for Singapore

Four pillars of professional security — built around trained people, proven processes, and the right technology for the job. Whether you need a single guard or a national event force, we scale to fit.

01 — Security Manpower Services

Trained Officers. Trusted Outcomes.

The foundation of every security deployment. Our PLRD-licensed officers are screened, trained, and supervised to deliver consistent protection across commercial, residential, industrial, healthcare, and hospitality environments.

  • PLRD Licensed & WSQ Certified — Every officer meets Singapore's stringent regulatory standards.
  • 24/7 Shift Coverage — Round-the-clock deployment with built-in redundancy.
  • Uniformed & Plainclothes — Visible deterrent or discreet protection, your choice.
  • Tiered Supervision — Site supervisors, area managers, and ops directors layered for accountability.
  • Background-Verified — Multi-stage screening, criminal record checks, and reference validation.
  • Continuous Training — Quarterly refreshers on incident response, customer service, and conflict de-escalation.
Best for: Commercial buildings · Condominiums (MCSTs) · Industrial sites · Retail · Hospitals · Schools · Government facilities
02 — Traffic Marshall Controls

Safe Flow. Zero Incidents.

Professional traffic management for construction sites, road works, special events, and school zones. Our LTA-trained marshalls coordinate vehicle and pedestrian movement with regulatory-compliant signage, equipment, and communication protocols.

  • LTA-Trained Marshalls — Certified for site traffic management under Singapore standards.
  • Full Equipment Provision — High-vis vests, traffic batons, cones, signs, and radio comms.
  • Risk Assessment Included — Pre-deployment site survey and traffic flow planning.
  • Public Liability Coverage — Comprehensive insurance protecting your project.
  • Construction Site Specialists — Experience with BCA-regulated worksites and contractor coordination.
  • Emergency Response Protocols — Direct escalation to supervisors and emergency services when needed.
Best for: Construction projects · Road resurfacing · Outdoor events · School pickup zones · Heavy equipment moves · Building handovers
03 — Major Event Security

Crowds Controlled. Events Protected.

End-to-end security planning and deployment for concerts, sporting events, festivals, corporate launches, and conventions. From a single VIP visit to a stadium-scale event, we handle the planning, the people, and the on-ground command.

  • Pre-Event Risk Assessment — Threat analysis, crowd modelling, and contingency planning before day one.
  • Crowd Management — Trained crowd controllers with experience at SG concerts, F1, sports, and festivals.
  • VIP & Close Protection — Discreet executive protection for principals, talent, and dignitaries.
  • SPF & SCDF Coordination — Established working relationships with Singapore Police and Civil Defence.
  • Access Control & Screening — Bag checks, ticket validation, accreditation zones, perimeter management.
  • On-Site Command Post — Mobile command centre with live ops director and incident logging.
Best for: Concerts & festivals · F1 / sporting events · Product launches · Conventions · Trade shows · Corporate galas · Public roadshows
04 — Security Consultancy

Independent Advice. Better Decisions.

Risk assessments, security audits, compliance advisory, and incident response planning from certified security professionals. We help organisations identify gaps, prioritise investments, and meet regulatory obligations — without selling them more than they need.

  • Physical Security Audits — Top-to-bottom site assessments with prioritised remediation roadmaps.
  • Risk & Threat Analysis — Quantitative threat modelling tailored to your industry and assets.
  • Compliance Advisory — PLRD, MOM, ISO 27001, BCA, and sector-specific frameworks.
  • Penetration Testing (Physical) — Authorised red-team exercises to test your real-world defences.
  • Incident Response Planning — Playbooks, escalation matrices, and table-top exercises.
  • Insider Threat Assessment — Behavioural and access-pattern analysis for high-security environments.
Best for: MNCs & HQ buildings · Critical infrastructure · Data centres · Healthcare · Financial institutions · Government agencies · Family offices
