Our Core Services

Four Pillars of Security & Manpower

Trained PLRD-licensed officers. Independent risk assessment. Integrated CCTV with 24/7 command centre. Specialist event and VIP coverage. The same four pillars Elitez has been delivering to MCSTs, MNCs, churches, and healthcare sites across Singapore.

Trained Officers. Strictly No Double Shift.

The foundation of every deployment. Our PLRD-licensed officers are screened, WSQ-trained via our captive Adept Academy, and paid one of the highest packages in the industry — because rested, well-paid officers deliver Deterrence, Observation & Reporting, Protection of Personnel/Property, and the Mitigation of Safety Risks our clients pay for.

  • 100% PLRD Licensed & WSQ Certified — including the WSQ "Going the Extra Mile" service-quality module.
  • Strictly No Double Shift — explicit company policy. No exceptions.
  • Buddy System & Back-Up Roster — minimises manpower issues at your site.
  • Tiered Supervision — site supervisors, area managers, and ops directors with mid-management backup regardless of contract value.
  • Welfare Floor — 7–14 days annual leave, 14 days medical, 60 days hospitalisation, NTUC union, quarterly $50 goodie packs, performance bonus, long-service award.
  • Formal Disciplinary Matrix — 21-offence framework with counselling → written warning → termination escalation.
Best for: MCSTs & condominiums · Commercial buildings · Industrial MNCs · Healthcare · Religious sites · Specialist roles (concierge, safe-entry, escort, VIP, loan-shark prevention).
02 — Security Audit & Risk Assessment

Independent Eyes. Costed Recommendations.

An evidence-based walk-through of your premises. We document the present level of security, identify gaps you may have stopped seeing, and submit a prioritised report — including authorised red-team and penetration exercises where the brief calls for it. Our consultants are independent: we will not over-spec a tech stack just to enlarge the contract.

  • Present-Level Security Walk-Through — full coverage of perimeter, access, vertical zones, BOH, and incident-response readiness.
  • Steps to Enhance — prioritised, costed remediation roadmap with quick-wins called out separately.
  • Recommendation & Report Submission — written brief delivered to your management or MCST committee.
  • Red-Teaming & Penetration Exercises — authorised physical-security audits (loading bay, after-hours, VMS bypass).
  • Compliance Advisory — PLRD, MOM, PWM, BCA, fire-safety, and PDPA touchpoints.
  • Insurance & Liability Mapping — gaps that may surface at claim time.
Best for: MCSTs evaluating renewals · MNC HQ buildings · Healthcare and eldercare sites · Religious institutions · Family offices · Pre-fitout commercial tenants.

One Console. One Accountable Team.

Free onsite risk assessment, free installation up to 250m of cabling, no upfront payment, full warranty for the contract period.

  • System Auditing & Consultation — assess your existing CCTV, intrusion, and access-control stack against current threat profile.
  • Upgrade & Enhancement — AI-CCTV, line-crossing analytics, license plate recognition, facial recognition, online facilities booking.
  • Comprehensive Maintenance — preventive and corrective with documented response SLAs.
  • Remote Video Monitoring — choice of onsite or offsite recording storage; PDPA-compliant retention.
