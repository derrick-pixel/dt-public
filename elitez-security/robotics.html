The Stack

Tech That Replaces Watch-Hours

We don't sell autonomous robots. We deploy proven, low-friction technology that lets one trained officer cover the work of two — backed by 24/7 monitoring from our Kallang command centre.

🎥

AI-CCTV with Line-Crossing

Cameras at strategic perimeters with intrusion alerts, motion analytics, and instant push to the command centre — no operator scanning idle feeds.

🚗

License Plate Recognition

Auto-gate entry for resident and pre-registered vehicles. Frees the gate guard for the actual job — visitor management and incident response.

📋

Visitor Management System

PDPA-compliant handheld, desktop, and self-registration kiosks. Resident pre-registration cuts gate processing time from 90 seconds to under 10.

🛡️

Virtual Patrol & Clocking

Replaces physical patrol clocking with checklist-driven CCTV walkthroughs. 17 virtual patrol stations on a single overnight shift, fully evidenced.

🆔

Facial Recognition Access

For staff-only zones, BOH corridors, and overnight side gates. Replaces card-tap (lost cards) and tail-gating risk with a hands-free flow.

🛰️

Anti-Exhaustion Fleet Device

For mobile patrol and supervisor cars. Detects fatigue, distraction, and smoking; ops centre issues live voice alerts via the integrated MDVR.
