Competitor Landscape

This admin view renders the competitor landscape from /data/competitors.json. It surfaces the top-5 threats with rationale, plus the full competitor database scored on threat level, beatability, monthly pricing in SGD, headquartered region, and website design rating. Search by name, value proposition, HQ city, or feature set; filter by category (global incumbent, SG local, regional challenger, DIY alternative, adjacent), HQ region, and minimum threat level. The data layer is produced by the 9-agent competitor-intel pipeline; the rest of the dashboard (insights, whitespace atlas, design audit, board report) is reachable from the top navigation.
