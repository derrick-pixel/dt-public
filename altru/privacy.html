1. Who We Are

Altru Asia Pte Ltd ("Altru", "we", "our") is a Singapore-incorporated company operating a wedding charitable gifting platform at altru.asia. We are a commercial fund-raiser under the Singapore Charities Act 1994. We are subject to the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA) as amended by the Personal Data Protection (Amendment) Act 2020 (effective 1 February 2021).

This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights.

3. Legal Basis for Collection

We rely on the following bases under the PDPA:

  • Consent — you provide explicit consent when registering or sending a gift. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 9).
  • Contractual necessity — processing your name, email, and mobile is necessary to deliver the service you have requested.
  • Legal obligation — collection of certain data (audit logs, sanctions screening records) is required by MAS Notice PSN01, the Charities Act, and the PDPA itself.

4. NRIC Collection

We collect NRIC/FIN numbers from couples only, for the sole purpose of IRAS tax receipt generation — enabling couples to claim the 250% tax deduction on IPC donations made in their name. NRIC collection is voluntary.

Your NRIC is:

  • Encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM before storage
  • Never logged in plaintext
  • Shared only with the relevant IPC charity's finance team for tax receipt purposes, with your consent
  • Never used for authentication (compliant with PDPC guidance effective 2027)

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and confirming your gift or wedding registration
  • Sending transactional communications (magic links, gift confirmations, refund notices)
  • Generating IRAS tax receipts for IPC donations (if NRIC provided)
  • AML/CFT compliance screening as required by MAS Notice PSN01
  • Maintaining an audit trail as required by the Charities Act (5-year retention)
  • Detecting and preventing fraud

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond what is described above.

8. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data in transit protected by TLS 1.2+
  • NRIC encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
  • Session tokens are HMAC-signed and SHA-256 hashed before storage
  • Admin access protected by Cloudflare Access (SSO)
  • Rate limiting on all authentication endpoints
  • No plaintext PII in system logs
  • Deployed on Cloudflare's globally distributed edge infrastructure

9. Your Rights Under the PDPA

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time (note: this may affect your ability to use the service)
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (subject to our legal retention obligations under the Charities Act)
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request via our data request form or email dpo@altru.asia. We will respond within 30 calendar days.

10. Submit a Data Request

Use the form below to submit a data access, correction, deletion, or consent withdrawal request. We will acknowledge within 2 business days and respond within 30 calendar days.

11. Data Breach Notification

If we determine that a data breach has occurred that is likely to result in significant harm to individuals, or that affects 500 or more individuals, we will:

  • Notify the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) within 3 calendar days of making that determination
  • Notify affected individuals as soon as reasonably practicable
  • Provide clear information on what happened, what data was affected, and what steps you can take

12. Cookies

Altru uses one cookie: altru_session — an HTTP-only, SameSite=Strict session cookie set only when a couple signs in via magic link. It contains no personal data; it is a signed opaque token. It expires 30 days after last use. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

13. Children

Our platform is for adults only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, contact dpo@altru.asia immediately.

14. Changes to This Policy

We will notify registered couples by email of any material changes to this policy at least 14 days before they take effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent version.
