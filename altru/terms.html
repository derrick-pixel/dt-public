5 · The gift lifecycle and the 14-day window

Every gift moves through a defined state machine:

  1. Pending. The gift has been received into Altru's escrow arrangement. The 14-day countdown starts at the timestamp of payment.
  2. Authorised. The couple has reviewed the gift and authorised release. Altru remits the charity portion to the partner charity's PayNow-UEN within one banking day.
  3. Auto-refunded. If the couple has not authorised release within 14 days, the gift is automatically refunded to the donor in full. This is the safety default of the platform.
  4. Donor-refunded. A donor may unilaterally cancel a gift within the 14-day window. After release, this right ends.
  5. Released. Funds disbursed to the charity. The transaction is final at this point and no longer reversible by Altru.
Important. Altru does not exercise any discretion in this flow. The state of a gift is determined by the actions of the parties (donor, couple) and by the passage of time. We provide the dashboard, the audit trail, and the automated execution.
