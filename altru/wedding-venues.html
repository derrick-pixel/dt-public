🗓️ Auspicious Wedding Dates 2026

Good-fortune dates per the Chinese Lunar Calendar. Popular dates at top venues book out 12–18 months ahead — plan early.

⚠️ Dates to avoid: 3rd Lunar Month (26 Mar–16 May 2026) · 7th Lunar Month Ghost Festival (13 Aug–10 Sep 2026) · Year Breaker Month (7 Dec 2026–4 Jan 2027). Venues may still be available on these dates but some families prefer to avoid them.

💡 Insider Tips Before You Sign

✓Visit on a banquet night — see the real setup, lighting and crowd flow before committing.
✓Ask about F&B minimum — some venues charge a minimum spend on top of the per-table price.
✓Clarify GST & service charge — always ask for the nett all-in price, not the ++ price.
✓Weekend premium — Saturday dinner slots can cost 15–25% more than a weekday dinner.
✓Menu tasting — reputable venues include a complimentary tasting — insist on it before signing.
✓Cancellation policy — read the deposit forfeiture clause carefully, especially for peak dates.
