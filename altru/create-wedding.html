Set up a wedding page

Giving to a couple who doesn't have an Altru page yet? Create one for them here — then send your gift. You'll get a link to forward to the couple so they can claim it.

How this works: You set up the page with just the couple's names and one mobile number. Your gift is held safely in Altru's escrow. After you pay, you'll get a claim link to forward to the couple (we also text it to them). Once they claim it, they decide whether to forward the charity portion. If the page isn't claimed within 90 days, every held gift is refunded in full.
One mobile number for either the groom or the bride. We text a verification code here when they claim — so it must be a number the couple controls. Singapore format, e.g. +6591234567.
