Compliance notes
----------------

Per the Malaysian Medical Advertising Board ("MAB") ruling on private
healthcare promotional material — and the internal MAB audit documented
at /compliance.html — all marketing imagery is constrained to:

  (a) facility / technology / environment shots, and
  (b) fully anonymised "professional anatomy" shots (gloved hands,
      back-of-head silhouettes) for any depiction of clinical staff.

No identifiable practitioner faces, no testimonial imagery, no
before/after patient shots, no price-comparison graphics appear on the
site. Named consultants appear as text only, with MMC registration
numbers to be confirmed prior to go-live.

See /images.html for the full image brief (slot-by-slot composition
guidance, MAB rationale, and replacement roadmap) and the recommended
SG/KL architectural photographers for Phase 2.
