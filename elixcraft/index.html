ELIXCRAFT is a gamified employee lifecycle system built on Singapore's SkillsFuture and MOM frameworks. Each employee is a Commander Unit — you choose your faction, configure your stats, and progress through career nodes on the Fog of War map.

01
PICK FACTION
Protoss (Engineering), Terran (Ops/Finance), or Zerg (Sales/People). Your faction sets your department track and stat bonuses.
→
02
CREATE UNIT
6-step onboarding: choose role, allocate STR / INT / CHA / DEX stats, equip skills (SkillsFuture-mapped), and select your benefits armour.
→
03
COMMAND CENTER
Your HQ. Monitor XP, resources, active quests, roster, and industry specialisation. Access the Career Map and SITREP analytics.
→
04
COMPLETE QUESTS
Daily and weekly objectives earn XP. Hit milestones to unlock fog-of-war career nodes and reveal new paths on the career map.
→
05
BOSS BATTLE
Performance review season. Survive the review, level up, unlock new roles, and see your SITREP analytics update in real time.
