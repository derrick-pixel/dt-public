ONBOARDING USER GUIDE
Factions · Stats · HR Portal · Career Map · Performance Review
📖 READ ⬇ PDF

ELIX ONBOARDING is a gamified employee lifecycle system built on Singapore's SkillsFuture and MOM frameworks. Each employee is a Team Member — you choose your faction, configure your stats, and progress through career nodes on the locked paths map.

01
PICK DEPARTMENT
Technology (Engineering), Operations (Ops/Finance), or Growth (Sales/People). Your department sets your career track and stat bonuses.
→
02
CREATE PROFILE
6-step onboarding: choose role, allocate STR / INT / CHA / DEX stats, equip skills (SkillsFuture-mapped), and select your benefits armour.
→
03
DASHBOARD
Your HQ. Monitor XP, resources, active objectives, roster, and industry specialisation. Access the Career Map and analytics.
→
04
COMPLETE OBJECTIVES
Daily and weekly objectives earn XP. Hit milestones to unlock locked career nodes and reveal new paths on the career map.
→
05
PERFORMANCE REVIEW
Performance review season. Survive the review, level up, unlock new roles, and see your analytics update in real time.
