CHAPTER 01

⬡ WHAT IS ELIXCRAFT?

ELIXCRAFT transforms the way employees and managers experience HR — turning the daily work of career management into a strategic, engaging command experience.

ELIX stands for Elitez Intelligence Xecution — the AI-powered HR intelligence engine that runs beneath ELIXCRAFT. Built on Singapore's SkillsFuture and MOM (Ministry of Manpower) frameworks, ELIXCRAFT reimagines the employee lifecycle as a real-time strategy game where every employee is a Commander Unit in a growing empire.

Whether you are an individual contributor tracking your KPIs and managing your leave, a team lead monitoring your squad's engagement and performance, or a senior manager analysing company-wide attrition and flight risk — ELIXCRAFT gives you the intelligence and tools to make better decisions, faster.

⬡ CORE PHILOSOPHY
Your career is not a ladder — it is a campaign. Each project completed, skill mastered, and milestone reached advances your Commander through levels, unlocks new career nodes, and strengthens your empire. ELIXCRAFT makes this journey visible, measurable, and rewarding.

THE TWO PORTALS

👤 EMPLOYEE PORTAL

Your personal command interface. Track KPIs as Mission Objectives, manage leave via PTO Shield, submit expense claims, get AI-powered HR guidance, and view your career progression path.

🏛️ EMPLOYER PORTAL

The strategic command layer. Access the full Unit Registry, monitor team headcount and hiring pipeline, run analytics across cost, demographics, engagement, talent pipeline, and risk.

HOW IT WORKS

Everything in ELIXCRAFT maps directly to real HR processes and employment data. There are no random elements or fictional mechanics. When your character gains XP, it reflects completing a real training programme. When you "unlock" a career node, it reflects meeting genuine competency requirements for a role. When you "equip armor," you are selecting your actual benefits package.

ℹ SINGAPORE CONTEXT
All leave entitlements, CPF contribution rates, salary bands, and employment terms displayed in ELIXCRAFT are based on current Singapore legislation and market benchmarks. The system is updated annually in line with MOM and CPF Board releases.

ELIXCRAFT is built for Elitez — a Singapore-based HR and recruitment firm — and reflects the actual industry sectors, role grades, and career frameworks used across their client portfolio spanning Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Sales, and Operations.

CHAPTER 04

📊 UNIT STATS — STR, INT, CHA, DEX

Every Commander in ELIXCRAFT is defined by four core stats. These stats are not arbitrary numbers — they are direct representations of professional competencies used in real performance appraisals, mapped to the ELIX Assessment Framework and SkillsFuture competency bands.

⚔️
STR
STRENGTH

The ability to lead, influence outcomes, and drive results through others. High STR Commanders are effective managers, change agents, and executives who can marshal resources and move organisations.

Real-world equivalents: Leadership ratings, management effectiveness, stakeholder influence, P&L ownership
🧠
INT
INTELLIGENCE

Technical depth, analytical thinking, and learning velocity. High INT Commanders are domain experts, data-driven decision-makers, and fast learners who can absorb complex systems and apply them effectively.

Real-world equivalents: Technical skills score, certifications earned, problem-solving assessments, code quality metrics
🗣️
CHA
CHARISMA

Communication clarity, stakeholder management, and the power of persuasion. High CHA Commanders build strong client relationships, inspire teams, and create alignment across diverse groups.

Real-world equivalents: Communication score, 360° feedback, client NPS, presentation effectiveness
⚡
DEX
DEXTERITY

Execution speed, adaptability under pressure, and time management. High DEX Commanders ship faster, pivot effectively when priorities change, and consistently meet deadlines even in ambiguous environments.

Real-world equivalents: Sprint velocity, on-time delivery rate, response time, agility assessment scores

HOW STATS ARE USED

CONTEXTSTAT EFFECT
Boss Battle (Performance Review)Your combined stat score determines attack power vs the review "boss" difficulty. Higher STR increases damage against leadership challenges; higher INT defeats technical challenges.
Quest CompletionQuests have hidden stat requirements. A "System Design Mastery" quest favours high INT; a "Stakeholder Presentation" quest favours high CHA.
Tech Tree UnlocksCertain career nodes require minimum stat thresholds. Principal Engineer requires INT ≥ 85; Engineering Manager requires STR ≥ 70.
Career Map Fog of WarUnexplored nodes reveal based on your level and relevant stat scores. Higher stats reveal more of the career landscape.
✓ STAT GROWTH
Stats grow naturally as you complete quests, earn certifications, and progress through appraisals. You can also boost specific stats by equipping Power-Up skills from SkillsFuture-approved training programmes. A "Distributed Systems" skill, for example, grants +20% INT in relevant contexts.

CHAPTER 05

⚙ CHARACTER CREATION — 6-STEP DEPLOYMENT

After selecting your faction, you enter the 6-Step Character Creation wizard. This represents your employee onboarding journey, mapping each step to a real HR process. Complete all 6 steps to deploy your Commander to the Command Center.

01
ROLE SELECTION
Choose your starting role from the available positions within your faction. Roles have level requirements, stat focuses, and salary bands displayed. Your choice sets your job title, reporting line, and initial career track. HR equivalent: Job offer acceptance & role assignment.
💡 You are not locked to your starting role forever. Role changes happen via the Tech Tree as you level up.
02
STAT ALLOCATION
Distribute 20 stat points across STR, INT, CHA, and DEX. Each faction starts with different base values. You can add to any stat but cannot reduce below the faction baseline. Points spent here represent your personal assessment at onboarding. HR equivalent: Pre-hire assessment scores & skills profile.
03
SKILL EQUIPMENT (POWER-UPS)
Select up to 4 starting skills from the SkillsFuture-mapped skill library. Skills are categorised by domain (Technical, Leadership, Communication, Operational) and each has a level rating (1–5). Higher-level skills require XP to unlock. HR equivalent: Skills declaration & SkillsFuture portfolio.
04
BENEFITS ARMOR
Configure your Benefits package by selecting tiers for Medical, Dental, Annual Leave, CPF, and optional extras. Medical has Gold/Silver/Bronze tiers. Annual Leave defaults to MOM statutory minimum and increases with tenure. HR equivalent: Benefits enrolment at onboarding.
🛡 CPF is mandatory for all Singapore Citizens and PRs and cannot be deselected. Equity (RSU) unlocks at Level 8.
05
INDUSTRY SPECIALISATION
Choose one of 16 Elitez industry sectors to specialise in. This unlocks sector-specific career nodes on the Career Map and determines which industry benchmarks your SITREP analytics compare against. HR equivalent: Industry specialisation declaration for placement purposes.
06
DEPLOY — ENTER COMMAND CENTER
Review your full Commander profile and click DEPLOY to save your character and enter the Command Center. Your profile is saved to browser localStorage and persists across sessions. HR equivalent: Contract signing & Day 1 onboarding completion.

CHAPTER 09

🏆 THE BOSS BATTLE — PERFORMANCE REVIEW

Twice a year, ELIXCRAFT enters Boss Battle Season — the Performance Review Cycle. This is the most consequential event in your Commander's journey. Your accumulated stats, quest completions, and skill loadout determine whether you win the battle, level up, and unlock promotions — or enter a PIP (Performance Improvement Plan).

HOW THE BATTLE WORKS

1
SELF-ASSESSMENT (PHASE 1)
Rate yourself on each competency. Your self-score becomes your opening move. Higher self-scores increase first-strike damage but must be backed by evidence.
2
MANAGER REVIEW (THE BOSS)
Your manager's ratings become the boss's attack score. High alignment between self and manager scores reduces damage taken. Large gaps increase the boss's power significantly.
3
CALIBRATION (BOSS PHASES)
Each calibration round is a boss phase. Your completed quests and certifications provide bonus attack rounds. The boss gets harder as you aim for higher grades.
4
OUTCOME
Win → Level up, receive XP bonus, unlock new career nodes, get salary increment. Draw → Maintain level, no increment. Loss → Enter PIP mode with 90-day recovery arc.

⚡ PRO TIPS

THE CAREER MAP — FOG OF WAR

The Career Fog of War Map is a spatial representation of all possible career paths from your current position. Explored nodes (visible) show roles you have held or are adjacent to. Unexplored nodes (fogged) reveal as you level up and meet prerequisite stat thresholds. The map is faction-specific and updates dynamically after each Boss Battle outcome.

CHAPTER 10

📖 GLOSSARY — GAME TERMS TO HR TERMS

ELIXCRAFT uses gamified language throughout, but every term maps precisely to an HR or employment concept. Use this reference whenever you encounter unfamiliar terminology.

COMMANDER UNIT
An individual employee. Each person in the organisation is represented as a deployable unit with stats, skills, and a career trajectory.
FACTION
Department / business unit. Protoss = Engineering, Terran = Operations/Finance/HR, Zerg = Sales/Marketing/People.
DEPLOY
Onboard / hire. Deploying a Commander = completing onboarding and becoming an active employee.
XP (EXPERIENCE POINTS)
Cumulative performance and learning credits. Earned through quest completion, training, and milestones. Drives level advancement.
QUEST BOARD
OKR / goal-tracking system. Main Quests = company OKRs; Side Quests = team projects; Training Quests = L&D programmes.
ARMOR (BENEFITS)
Employee benefits package. Medical, dental, annual leave, CPF, and equity slots represent your full compensation and benefits envelope.
POWER-UPS (SKILLS)
Certifications and competencies. Each Power-Up corresponds to a SkillsFuture-mapped skill with a proficiency level (1–5 = basic to mastery).
BOSS BATTLE
Performance appraisal cycle. A structured, turn-based review where your stats and quest completions are evaluated against grade expectations.
TECH TREE
Career progression framework. Shows all available role transitions from your current position, with unlock requirements for each node.
PTO SHIELD
Paid Time Off / Leave entitlements. Shield "durability" = remaining leave days. Annual, Medical, Childcare, and other leave types mapped to MOM guidelines.
RESOURCE REQUISITION
Expense claims and reimbursements. Submit receipts for training, transport, equipment, and meals within your annual claim allowance.
GHOST SLOT
Open headcount position. A budgeted vacancy that has not yet been filled. Clicking a Ghost Slot opens the recruitment workflow.
SUPPLY
Headcount utilisation. Current deployed units vs maximum approved headcount. Supply > 90% triggers a hiring recommendation alert.
SITREP / INTELLIGENCE HUB
Analytics and reporting dashboard. "Situation Report" — shows personal performance and company-wide HR intelligence in real time.
FLIGHT RISK
Attrition risk score (0–100). AI-generated score per employee based on engagement trends, pay compression, and tenure signals.
TACTICAL ADVISOR
HR Policy AI. Trained on Singapore MOM, CPF Board, SkillsFuture, and Elitez internal policy. Answers any HR or employment question.
UNIT REGISTRY
Employee directory. Searchable roster of all active units with profiles, department filters, and performance data.
COMMAND CONFIGURATION
HR Settings. Administrative configuration for Departments, Hiring workflows, Appraisal cycles, Leave policies, and Salary structures.
