JR+ Reframe

Reread of the WSG WDG(JR+) CFP spec (12 Jan 2026, ref WSG-ENTE-PRO-42-9-2-R1) shows Component 3 has narrower scope than v2 implied — diagnostic tools + L&D systems only, must bundle with C1 or C2, mandatory JRET/HCDT outcome. v3 reframes the 9+8 portfolio around three outcome-led JR+ engagement archetypes plus a parallel PSG/self-serve track for products that don't fit JR+. v2 archive · v1 archive.

The JR+ Reality Check

Three constraints from the WSG CFP spec (12 Jan 2026) that determine how the 9+8 portfolio can actually be funded. v2 was loose on this; v3 fixes it.

Three Outcome-Led JR+ Archetypes

Stop selling "9 workflows + 8 platforms". Sell engagements with measurable JR outcomes — the products are the implementation layer, not the headline. Each archetype maps to specific JR+ components and produces a JRET- or HCDT-measurable outcome.

JRET / HCDT — Mandatory Deliverables

Per the CFP spec p4, every WDG(JR+) project must (a) increase job value via JRET OR (b) improve human capital maturity via HCDT, AND demonstrate that local employees benefited from redesigned jobs. Each archetype must produce evidence against these criteria. Below is the deliverable matrix.

Reframing · Stretching the JR+ Net

Parallel Track · PSG / Self-Serve

Products that genuinely don't fit JR+ go on a parallel track — PSG-eligible (50% subsidy) or self-pay. This is the "bite-size for micro-SMEs" wedge: transparent prices, no consult required, immediate value. Once a relationship exists, qualified prospects upgrade into a JR+ archetype.

Per-Platform Competitor Benchmarks

For each of the 8 platforms (and the 9-workflow stack), the SG-relevant competitor set, indicative aggregate price, our price, and annual customer savings vs the alternative they would otherwise license. Used inside proposals to anchor the "vs your current SaaS stack" narrative.

Strategic Recommendations · v3 Shifts

Three top-level moves that follow from the JR+ reality check. Cancers to watch: claiming Component 3 covers everything, pricing à la carte products inside JR+ proposals, ignoring the dual-track funnel.
