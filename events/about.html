Who we are

A creative + operational studio
born inside an HR ecosystem.

From staffing supermarkets in 2010 to crafting 1,500-pax themed dinners in 2025 — Elitez Events represents the creative pulse of Elitez Group.

Vision & Mission

People-centred experiences,
powered by purpose and creativity.

Our Vision

To be Asia's most vibrant force in experiential events — redefining engagement through creativity, purpose, and human connection.

Our Mission

We craft bold, people-centred experiences that energise and connect. Driven by purpose and powered by creativity, we turn every moment into lasting impact.

Our journey

From manpower roots to meaningful moments.

2010
Founded as a Promoter Agency — specialised in deploying temp staffing to supermarkets and FMCG accounts.
2013
Grew into a full-suite HR service provider. Recruitment, HR consultancy and temp staffing solutions for Retail, F&B and FMCG sectors.
2018
Elitez FMCG growth in merchandising and on-ground activations for promoter management.
2021
Elitez FMCG launched Elitez Events — a strong team of creative and passionate personnel pursuing the fruits of events management.
2026
35+ recurring clients across SG enterprise, government, healthcare, hotels and lifestyle. From 30-pax retreats to 1,500-pax CNY company dinners.
