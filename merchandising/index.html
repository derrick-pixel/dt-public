EMR-Intel — bundled, not sold

Your field force, instrumented.

EMR-Intel is the platform that makes the bench legible to your brand team. Every visit is GPS-stamped, every planogram check is photographed, every out-of-stock event is logged in real time. Your dashboard shows MSL compliance, share-of-shelf, share-of-display, promo compliance, and daily OOS trend without anyone exporting a spreadsheet.

The mobile app ships in English, Chinese, and Bahasa Melayu — non-trivial for a Singapore-Malaysia field force.
