# Elitez Merchandising — Marketing Site

Singapore's leading FMCG in-store merchandiser-promoter agency. A business unit of [Elitez Group of Companies](https://elitez.com.sg).

**Target domain (production):** merchandising.elitez.com.sg
**Live (preview):** https://derrick-pixel.github.io/merchandising/
**Stack:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (per dt-site-creator `static-informational` archetype)
**Methodology:** [dt-site-creator v3.0.1](https://github.com/derrick-pixel/dt-site-creator)

## What this site does

Presents Elitez Merchandising's two service models (Dedicated, Shared), the proprietary EMR-Intel field-force management platform, tier-1 client outcomes, and routes qualified FMCG brand + retail leads to Jack Wang, Associate Director.
