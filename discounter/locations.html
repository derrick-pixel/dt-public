The #1 hub for Filipino domestic workers — estimated 80,000 FDWs from the Philippines gather here on Sundays. Money transfer, Filipino food, hair salons, agencies.

Peninsula Plaza

111 North Bridge Road
Popular with Myanmar and Filipino FDWs. Packed on Sundays with personal services, food stalls, remittance shops and community gatherings.
