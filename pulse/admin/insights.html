TAM → SAM → SOM, then four personas with willingness-to-pay bands.

Two artefacts on one page. Market sizing decomposes the SG SME marketing-services opportunity into TAM (every SG SME's annual marketing wallet), SAM (the 55,000 SMEs already DIY-ing across 8+ tools / freelancers / micro-agencies — the bundle-consolidation buyer), and SOM (the 3-year capture for the ops-stack-arbitrage Pulse bundle). The pricing strategy half profiles the four buyer personas, surfaces willingness-to-pay bands derived from competitor price points and the in-house-hire baseline, and recommends the three tiers (Lite / Core / Enterprise) that map to the buyer set. Four personas with structured NBA arithmetic

Each persona's nba.monthly_sgd_equivalent is the displaced-spend baseline. The expected WTP must sit between 0.4× and 1.2× that number, or the persona is flagged exploratory.
