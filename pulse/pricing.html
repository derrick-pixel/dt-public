Pricing questions

Before the sales call.

Why a price range instead of one number?
Your starting price depends on brand maturity, shot complexity, and ad budget scale. After the diagnostic we lock a specific number; we don't drift within the range without talking to you.
What if I need something not in the tier?
We carve it in as a one-off or add a line-item add-on. Website builds, brand refreshes, and campaign sprints are the most common examples. Quoted before work starts, billed as a separate one-off.
Do you bill in USD?
All retainers bill in SGD. We can quote USD or MYR for convenience, but the invoice + tax will be SGD. Malaysian clients are welcome — the contract still routes through the SG entity.
What does "30-day notice cancellation" actually mean?
You email us to cancel. 30 days later, the retainer ends. We hand over assets, logins, and the monthly ROI up to that point. No fee, no negotiation, no retention call loop.
Can I pause instead of cancel?
Yes — up to 2 months per calendar year. You pay a 25% holding fee during pause to keep your slot on the content calendar. Past 2 months, we cancel and you re-onboard if you come back.
