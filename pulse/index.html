Frequently ducked questions

Objections, answered.

What's the minimum term?
No minimum. Retainers bill monthly and you can cancel with 30 days' notice — in the contract, not the fine print. The first 90 days are usually where a retainer proves itself, so most clients stay beyond that.
Who actually does the work?
A Singapore-based account owner runs the retainer and closes the loop with you. An in-house craft team inside Elitez Group executes (design, video, ads, content). An AI layer handles repetitive scaffolding — first-draft copy, alt text, scheduling, QA. Because we sit inside a 350-person group, you're not paying for agency rent or senior-AM overhead — that saving shows up in your price.
How do you measure ROI?
One-page monthly report with three things: leads generated (qualified, not clicks), cost per lead vs. last month, and revenue attributed to Pulse-touched campaigns. We pick the metrics in week 1 with you; we don't cherry-pick vanity numbers.
What if I already have a freelancer?
Great — we can plug into an existing setup, or we can replace a patchwork of freelancers with a single retainer. Where we consistently beat freelancers: accountability, reporting, and the ability to ship 4 videos + 20 posts + a landing page in the same month without 4 separate invoices.
Will you work with tiny SMEs under 10 headcount?
Honestly, probably not yet. Under 10 staff with a deal size under $1K, a $500 freelancer is the right call. The ROI Diagnostic will tell you this directly — we'd rather not waste your retainer than take a doomed client.
How fast can you start?
Diagnostic call within 2 days of you reaching out. Onboarding (brand audit, tool access, content calendar) inside 5 working days. First content shipping by end of week 2. First ROI report by end of month 2.
Do you do one-off projects?
Retainers are our model — that's how we keep prices low. We do carve out one-offs for existing retainer clients (a website build, a brand refresh, a campaign sprint). New clients: start on a retainer, carve out from there.
What makes Elitez Group the right parent?
Elitez is 15+ years old, 350+ staff, 90+ client operations across SG / MY / KL. Pulse is our internal marketing team opened up as a revenue unit — so the retainer funding our own brand is the exact same retainer you're buying. Battle-tested on our own P&L before we sold it outside.
