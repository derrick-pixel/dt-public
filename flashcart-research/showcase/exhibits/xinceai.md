## Verdict

Reference implementation for the whitespace atlas pattern. The strategy canvas radar, 10×8 heatmap with tri-colour banding, and attack-plan cards were designed here first and back-ported to this template. Obsidian-with-orange-accent palette makes the artefacts feel editorial.

## What to steal

- Headline thesis copy on the strategy canvas.
- Click-to-reveal cell detail with pair-specific specialisation notes.
- ARTIFACT 01 / 02 / 03 labels with monospace accent.

## What not to steal

- Dark-mode-only palette — reskin via `:root` tokens for your brand.
