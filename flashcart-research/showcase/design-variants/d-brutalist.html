FAQ

Why so much cheaper than McKinsey?

McKinsey runs 30+ expert interviews and stages partner workshops. We use AI agents over a structured rubric and ship in 5 days. Different cost structure, same buying job for businesses that don't need partner-level interviews.

Why more expensive than a Frost & Sullivan report?

That report sells to your competitor too. Ours profiles your 30 competitors, sizes your SAM, designs your three pricing tiers. Bespoke to you, not the category.

Will the AI hallucinate?

Every claim is sourced. Every multiplier in the market sizing has a citation or is explicitly flagged "estimated; to be validated." Operator reviews every artefact before delivery. Full rework guarantee covers the edge case.

Can I run it myself?

Yes. The template is open-source MIT-licensed. GitHub repo here. You're paying for the operator's editorial — knowing which 30 competitors to pick, where heatmap dimensions sit, what counts as a credible attack plan vs aspirational fluff.

What if my market is outside SG/HK?

Phase 1 is SG & HK only because we know those markets cold (regulatory, grants, buyer behaviour). Phase 2 expands to MY/TH/PH/VN/ID at adjusted regional pricing.
