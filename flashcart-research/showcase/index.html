§ I — The mid-market gap

Every alternative is a different shape than the buyer wants.

SG HR Engagement Leads at MNCs assemble engagement events from four vendor lines every year — production, vouchers, photo booth, lucky-draw inventory. Nobody packages this as a single onsite-commerce activation. The category is empty because each incumbent owns one slice and won't bend into the others.

Big SI / agency

Pico, INX, Bray Leino Splash

Owns venue, AV, stage. No retail DNA, no PayNow rail, no inventory model. The threat is they bolt flashcart onto a D&D — that's where we win on speed-to-market, not on production scale.

A flashcart pop-up shows up at your event. Brand-sponsor inventory at sample / wholesale prices for staff. PayNow QR mobile checkout. Post-event GMV + attendance report. The HR team ends the year with measurable engagement uplift and one invoice instead of four.
