§ IV — Honest about what flashcart isn't

Built by retail operators, not by event production agencies.

Flashcart is not Pico. We don't own venue, AV, or stage. We're additive: we plug into the event you're already running, bring the inventory rail and the checkout, and report on what happened. Choose Pico for production. Choose flashcart for the engagement uplift the production was supposed to deliver.

What flashcart does

Brand-funded sample inventory, PayNow QR checkout, on-site pop-up curation, post-event reporting. The throughput layer: parts of the event that historically required vouchers + booth + lucky-draw prizes assembled separately.

What flashcart doesn't

We don't run AV, stage, lighting, MC, or catering. We don't ship vouchers post-event (use Giftano). We don't do photo booths (use Vivid Snaps). We're the inventory + checkout + reporting layer, not the production layer.

How brand sponsors pay

Brands buy sampling reach into MNC offices: a sponsored category slot per event, a quarterly catalogue brand co-marketing campaign, or annual exclusivity in a category (e.g. only one beauty brand per quarter). Brand contracts run separate from host contracts.

100% rework guarantee

If the post-event GMV + attendance + NPS don't beat the prior year's lucky-draw + goodie-bag baseline, we rework Year-2 free. If still no uplift, the Annual Engagement contract refunds for the events that underperformed.

§ V — Frequently asked

The questions HR + EX buyers ask.

Who pays for the inventory at Single Event?

Brand sponsors. Flashcart sells sampling reach to FMCG, beauty, and lifestyle brands. Hosts get the experience for free; brands pay for the audience.

Is Quarterly tier really WHG-claimable?

Wellness-fair structure qualifies under SG Workplace Health Grant. We provide the documentation pack at billing. Effective S$1,250/qtr post-PSG when claim approved.

What if we use Pico already?

Keep using Pico for AV + production. Flashcart slots in alongside as the inventory + checkout + reporting layer. The two integrate; we send our pop-up team in 90 mins before doors open.

Will my staff actually buy stuff?

Yes — brand-sample pricing means SKUs are 40–70% under retail. Average GMV per attendee at MNC pilots ranges S$25–80. We share comp data at the scoping call.

What about MNC compliance / procurement?

Standard NDA + corporate vendor onboarding. We're invoiced through one MNC vendor record (replaces the four prior records). PCI-DSS compliant payments through PayNow QR partner.

What if our event is outside SG?

Phase 1 is Singapore only. Phase 2 expands to MY (Q3 2026) and HK (Q4 2026) at adjusted regional pricing. ID/TH/VN are 2027 because country-readiness for premium HR tooling is currently 2–3.

Can a brand sponsor become an exclusive partner?

Yes — category exclusivity sold separately. One beauty brand per quarter, one FMCG per quarter, one lifestyle per quarter. Year-1 slots booked by application; talk to us early.

How do I share with my MNC HQ?

Quarterly attendance + NPS + GMV reporting deck (Annual tier). Drop into your engagement-program review with HQ. The deck is built around the metrics MNC HR HQ already tracks.
