Pricing principles

Four rules behind the ladder.

How we set the price and why the per-seat is constant.

PRINCIPLE 01

Swipe-card friendly

Up to roughly 50–100 seats, you should not need procurement to involve finance.

PRINCIPLE 02

Constant per-seat

S$2.99 per user, per month, across every paid tier. The platform-flat fee is what scales for capability.

PRINCIPLE 03

Month-to-month default

No annual lock-in on Starter, Team or Pro. Annual discounts are negotiated privately on Scale and Enterprise.

PRINCIPLE 04

Your content stays yours

You retain ownership of uploaded decks and AI-generated derivatives. We licence them solely to deliver training to your team.

Partner channel

Building a Learn module inside your HRMS or EOR stack?

Enterprise tier includes white-label, custom domain, SSO, HRIS integrations and a dedicated Customer Success Manager. Channel partners get co-marketing collateral and a partner-margin discount on the platform fee. Per-seat stays at S$2.99 so end-customer economics remain predictable.

Pricing FAQ

Questions on the ladder.

Minimum seats, trial limits, upgrades, downgrades, content ownership, billing currency.

No annual lock-in on Starter, Team or Pro. Month-to-month is the default. Annual commitments are negotiated privately on Scale and Enterprise where the discount mechanic is more typical.
Starter is free up to 5 seats with no time limit. You get read-only access to the Elitez library so you can evaluate the SG-compliance baseline before deciding on a paid tier.
Upgrades take effect immediately and are pro-rated for the remainder of the billing month. Downgrades take effect at the next billing month. You can move between Team, Pro, Scale and Enterprise without re-onboarding learners or losing analytics history.
Team and Pro require a minimum of 8 paid seats. Scale requires a minimum of 30 seats. Enterprise requires a minimum of 50 seats. The platform-flat fee on Pro, Scale and Enterprise is independent of seat count.
You do. You retain ownership of uploaded decks and any AI-generated derivatives. We licence the material solely to deliver training to your team on our platform. If you cancel, you can export everything and we remove the data on request.
Singapore Dollars (SGD) by default. We can bill in USD on Enterprise for channel-partner deployments where the partner prefers USD invoicing.
We do not publish a non-profit or education tier. Get in touch — if your use case is registered-charity or accredited institution, we will price against the platform fee on a case-by-case basis.
Month-to-month tiers renew on the billing date at the then-current published list price. Price changes are communicated 60 days in advance. No automatic step-ups without your confirmation.
