§ I · THE ATLAS

Plot every skill. Date every certification. Justify every dollar.

Elitez LMS treats your organisation's skill landscape the way an atlas treats geography: as something to survey, label, and revisit. Not a one-off training event — a living map.

What the atlas does

Pulls in your existing course library — slides, PDFs, screencasts — and pins each piece to a TSC unit. From day one, your HR director can answer: who is at which level, on which competency, and what's the next certification gap?

  • BYO-deck ingestion · PPT, PDF, DOCX
  • Auto-mapping to TSC v3.2 (39 sectoral frameworks)
  • Per-learner transcripts, exportable as PDF or signed CSV
  • Manager dashboard with red/amber/green coverage map
  • Quarterly grant-readiness audit, generated automatically

Where it sits

Between your existing HRIS and the SWDA training registry. We don't replace either — we make them comprehensible to each other.

§ III · PARTNERSHIPS & PROVENANCE

Built with the agencies your auditor recognises.

Elitez Group has been a Singapore-registered employer-of-record and training partner since 2008. Elitez LMS inherits those relationships, those audit trails, and that conservatism.

SWDA Continuing Professional Development

Every TSC unit completed on Elitez LMS counts toward CPD hours for SWDA's eight regulated tracks. The certificate carries a verifiable SWDA reference number.

SkillsFuture Credit eligibility

Selected enterprise modules are SkillsFuture-eligible at 70% subsidy for SMEs. Our admin console flags eligibility per learner per quarter.

PDPA & data residency

Singapore datacentre (Equinix SG3). All learner data resides locally. Notification protocols filed with PDPC. DPO published.

BCA / WSG / IRAS interop

Export bundles for BCA CORENET training records, WSG WDG(JR+) substantiation, and IRAS tax-deduction substantiation under §14R.
