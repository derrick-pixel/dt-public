How to read this contact sheet

BUCKET A · GAMIFIED · 3 VARIANTS

Heroes Academy (character-based RPG), Quest Board (quarterly missions + XP), Tournament Arena (team-vs-team broadcast). Use if you want the platform to drive participation through play; useful for client-companies that need quarterly prize structures.

BUCKET B · PROFESSIONAL · 2 VARIANTS

Skills Atlas (TSC roadmap-centric) and State Bulletin (audit-grade compliance). Both lean into the SWDA / SkillsFuture / PDPA story. Use if your buyer is the HR director who reads it before signing.

BUCKET C · REGIONAL / GLOBAL · 2 VARIANTS

Pan-Asia Platform (ASEAN-8 regional with multilingual + multi-currency) and Global Stratum (Fortune-500 world-class). Use if the next sales conversation is with a holding group with multiple geographies.
