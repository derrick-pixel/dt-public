§ I · Statement of Purpose

An LMS for quiet, compliant, year-end-defensible training.

Elitez LMS is designed for the Singapore HR director who has been asked, in the third week of November, to substantiate every dollar of training spend against PDPA, SkillsFuture, and SWDA reporting. Our position: training software should produce records first, dashboards second.

What we will, and will not, build into your training stack.

Elitez LMS will ingest your existing decks — Powerpoint, PDF, Word — and transform them into modular lessons with autogenerated quizzes. We will publish each learner's completion against the relevant TSC unit, retain that record for at least seven years, and produce on request an evidence pack suitable for SkillsFuture, IRAS §14R, or WSG WDG(JR+) substantiation.

We will not gamify your training without permission. We will not surface engagement metrics in lieu of substantive completion. We will not host data outside Singapore. We will not require an integration with a third-party identity provider that resides outside the PDPA-jurisdiction perimeter.

Our pricing is published below as a fee schedule, not a sales table. Tiered usage is metered transparently and invoiced monthly in Singapore Dollars, exclusive of GST.
