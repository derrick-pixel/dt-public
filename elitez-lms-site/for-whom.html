Segment 1

EA-licensed recruitment agencies (15–150 staff).

MOM EA Act, TAFEP, Fair Consideration, CPF and work-pass content ship out of the box. Role tracks for Recruitment Consultant, BD Executive, Account Manager and HR Operations are baseline. New recruiters land in a structured plan from day one; auditors get completion evidence in one click. Typical fit: Team or Pro tier.

PAIN

8–12 week recruiter ramp

Compliance audit asks for completion evidence. Role onboarding lives in eight disconnected decks per role. Recruiter ramp is 8–12 weeks of fragmented training.

SG SMEs and mid-market teams with decks already written.

Consultancies, advisories, law firms, design studios, professional-services partnerships, brand-led retail and F&B operators. Your onboarding deck, SOP pack and brand book already exist — they live in SharePoint or Google Drive and nobody reads them. Drop them into Pro; we return structured modules and AI-generated quizzes against your content within hours.

PAIN

Decks exist; nobody reads them

Not for us

Who this platform is not built for.

Plain about the boundary so neither side wastes a call.

Anti-audience
  • Deskless or frontline-only workforces. We are browser-based and English-first. EdApp and Disprz cover that shape better — go to them for shift-worker delivery, not us.
  • Enterprises above ~5,000 seats. If your Cornerstone, Workday or SAP SuccessFactors procurement is already in flight, finish that path. We are sized for SMEs and mid-market.
  • SMS-OTP-only or no-email workforces. Authentication is email-OTP today; SSO on Enterprise. Workforces without email addresses are not a fit.
  • Buyers who only want a catalogue. If you want to buy Coursera or Udemy seats and forward links, we are the wrong shape. We are an authoring + delivery platform, not a content marketplace.
