Role tracks

Five tracks ship with the platform.

Recruitment Consultant, BD Executive, Account Manager, HR Operations, and a Default track. On Scale and above, you build additional tracks per job family. Tracks are audience-scoped — non-admin staff see only what is keyed to their role.

M1

Month 1 — Compliance baseline

PDPA, TAFEP, AI-use-at-work, Employment Act Part IV, KETs. Every joiner across every track lands on the same compliance floor by Day 30.

M2

Month 2 — Role craft

Track-specific content: Boolean and candidate interview for Recruiters, BANT/MEDDIC and proposal for BD, retention and renewal for AM, payroll and termination for HR Ops.

M3

Month 3 — First production output

First qualified shortlist for Recruiters, first signed proposal for BD, first retention save for AM, first month-end run for HR Ops. Manager dashboard turns green.

Manager view

RAG progress, per-module breakdown, per-question heatmaps.

Managers see M1 / M2 / M3 progress in red-amber-green against their direct reports. On Pro and above, per-module and per-question analytics surface which content needs work. On Scale, knowledge-gap heatmaps show which questions teams consistently get wrong. Exports run to CSV from Pro; API from Scale.

AI literacy slab

Ship an AI policy your D&O carrier will recognise.
