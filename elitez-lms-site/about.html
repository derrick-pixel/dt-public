Story

How this platform got built.

Three steps from a SharePoint folder to a productised LMS.

2008–2022

Elitez Group operates in Singapore HR services

Staffing, consulting and outsourcing. Our own training content piles up in SharePoint and Google Drive.

2023–2024

We build an internal LMS for the ~200-strong Elitez team

The reverse-engineering pipeline shortens content authoring from weeks to hours.

2025 onwards

SG SMEs we work with ask whether they can use it for their own decks

We harden the platform, write a multi-tenant tier ladder and open the door.
