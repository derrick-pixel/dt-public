Editorial takeaways

What the data actually says.

01

CSD elasticity is dead past 15%.

Coca-Cola and Pepsi see linear uplift up to 15% promo depth. Anything deeper buys ego, not volume. Trade marketing should redirect that budget into facings, not blowouts.

02

Donki is its own category.

Korean ready-meals and Japanese imports show up to S$2 spread vs the same SKU at NTUC. Donki shoppers are not price-checking — they're browsing. Premium packs win.

03

Festival promo bunching costs share.

During CNY weeks 4–6, three-quarters of confectionery brands are on promo simultaneously. A counter-cyclical full-margin strategy in week 3 captures unsubsidised volume.
