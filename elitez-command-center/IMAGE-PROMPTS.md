Generate a set of **13 separate photographs** for the **Elitez Integrated Command Center**, a Singapore B2B security-technology operator that runs a centralised security-operations command centre as a managed service. Output 13 distinct images, one for each numbered brief below, at the aspect ratio stated.

SHARED STYLE — applies to all 13 photographs. Institutional corporate-documentary photography: real, sharp and credible, in the register of a Singapore government-agency or corporate annual-report shoot — never stock-cheesy, never editorial-warm, never sci-fi. Cool colour grade matched to a deep-navy brand palette (navy #00337a, ink #14233b, cool paper-grey #eef1f5, steel blue-grey #5a6b85): cool white balance, restrained saturation, gentle contrast, clean lifted blacks. Tiny accent glints of teal #0e8a9e, amber #d98a2b or green #15875a are allowed only as small practical lights (status LEDs, one screen highlight) — never a colour wash. Full-frame camera look. People are a contemporary multi-ethnic Singapore workforce (Chinese, Malay, Indian; mixed gender; ages 25–50), composed and focused — not smiling-stock — wearing smart-casual corporate clothing with subtle ID lanyards, or clean neutral navy/grey operational uniforms with no weapons. Composition is clean and intentional with calm negative space, strong horizontals, no dutch tilt. Subtle post: no heavy vignette, no HDR halo, no lens flare. Across every image avoid: warm or orange grade, golden-hour light, sci-fi holograms, neon, glitch or cyberpunk effects, glowing futuristic UI, fake data gibberish, classified map markings, weapons, military camouflage, surveillance-dystopia mood, posed thumbs-up poses, AI-warped hands or faces, visible third-party brand logos, and any baked-in text, captions or watermarks.
