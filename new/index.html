The shift

I didn't hire consultants. I learned to build.

For sixteen years I've been in the business of people — placing them, managing them, building careers and companies around them. Manpower. Recruitment. HR. The most human industry there is.

Then AI arrived, and every HR leader I knew felt the same quiet pressure: you are expected to lead your organisation's transformation into the AI era — and most of us had never written a line of code or shipped a model.

"So I stopped waiting for a vendor to sell me the future. In nine months I built an AI department inside Elitez — and started rebuilding the company with my own hands."

What began as curiosity became a discipline. Sales enablement, retail-shelf vision, AI-assisted job redesign, a learning platform, an operations command centre — function after function, I rebuilt how the work gets done. Some of it broke. Most of it stuck.

I'm not a futurist with a deck. I'm an operator with receipts — and now I help other HR and SME leaders make the same leap, without the fear and without the consultant tax.

And this isn't my first transformation. I steered Elitez through the COVID crisis and led its ESG transformation before AI. See the full transformation track record →
