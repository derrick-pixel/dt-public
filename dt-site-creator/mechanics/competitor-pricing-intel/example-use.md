# Past use — competitor-pricing-intel

## Elitez EOR (elix-eor) — **flagship reference implementation**
- **Grid size**: 15×15
- **Competitors**: 45+ (scraped real SG pricing where public, flagged estimates)
- **Categories**: global EOR · regional SEA EOR · SG payroll software · SG payroll outsourcing · SG accounting firms with embedded payroll · HR-tech startups · corporate secretarial · niche specialists · DIY alternatives
- **Strategy canvas dims**: Price · SG expertise · Compliance depth · Benefit stacking · Tech integration · Onboarding speed · Human support · Multi-country
- **White space identified**:
  1. **Micro F&B with foreign workers** — global EORs skip <10 headcount; SG payroll SaaS doesn't handle WP quota logic
  2. **Solo founder → first-hire** — no competitor serves the "zero-to-one" hiring journey with hand-holding + compliance together
  3. **Seasonal / gig-heavy operators** — every competitor assumes static payroll; none handles spike hiring + fast offboarding
- **GTM moves that followed**: S$59/emp founder rate · CardUp integration for card-miles stacking · bundled group healthcare at $25/mo

## Elitez Marketing Services (Elitez-marketing-services)
- **Grid size**: 8×8
- **Competitors**: 25 (mix of SG/regional PR and marketing agencies)
- **Strategy canvas dims**: Price · Service breadth · Industry specialism · Retainer flexibility · In-house vs outsourced · Speed · Reporting depth · Strategy-level involvement
- **White space identified**: "Mid-market SG SMEs wanting founder-level strategic involvement at agency pricing" — mostly served by either founder-time consultants (expensive, no delivery) or cheap freelancer agencies (no strategy)
- **GTM moves**: 3-tier retainer (S$799 / $2,499 / $4,499) with founder-led strategy hour per month in mid tier

## XinceAI (xinceai)
- **Grid size**: ~10×8 (AI workflow vendors don't have neat consumer-facing segmentation yet)
- **Competitors**: 30 across AI workflow vendors, Zapier/Make, local SG systems integrators, Big 4 digital transformation arms, in-house build
- **Strategy canvas dims**: Price · SG localisation · Pre-built workflows · WhatsApp native · Xero native · Setup speed · Customisation · Grant eligibility

## What to avoid (seen in past projects)

- **"All competitors" scope creep** → pick real threats, not every theoretical player. 25–50 is the sweet spot; more dilutes the analysis.
- **Fake pricing** → once a benchmark is faked, every downstream artifact is poisoned. Always scrape or mark `ESTIMATED`.
- **Treating it as a one-shot** → the atlas rots. Put a calendar reminder; refresh quarterly for fast-moving markets, semi-annually otherwise.
- **Publishing it publicly** → strategy goes behind the auth gate. Only the *distilled positioning* goes on the website.
- **8×8 when you needed 15×15** → if you find yourself sub-dividing "SME" into 3 sub-types during the analysis, you needed 15×15 from the start.
