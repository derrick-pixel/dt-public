§ V — Honest about the AI

Built by humans amplified by AI.

This isn't a McKinsey deck. It also isn't ChatGPT slop. It's a structured methodology run by an experienced operator with AI agents doing the parallel labour — the same way modern law firms use eDiscovery, the same way modern radiologists use AI screening. The agents don't replace judgement; they amortise the work that used to take consultants 6 weeks down to 5 days.

What the AI agents do

Parallel competitor research, source citation, market-size triangulation, scoring rubric application, draft generation. The throughput layer — the part that used to require a team of three associates.

What the operator does

Picks the right 30 competitors. Calibrates heatmap dimensions to your buyer's actual evaluation criteria. Decides which attack plan is realistic vs aspirational. Reviews every claim before it ships.

How you can verify

Every claim, multiplier, and score is traceable to a source — sitting in a Methodology Appendix at the back of the PDF and inside a structured JSON schema you can audit. MBB decks rarely show their working. This always does.

100% rework guarantee

If the deliverable doesn't meet your team's bar at the readout call, you get a full rework at no charge. If it still doesn't meet your bar after rework, full refund. The methodology is structured precisely so we can fix what's wrong without redoing what's right.

§ VI — Frequently asked

The questions everyone asks.

Why so much cheaper than McKinsey?

McKinsey runs 30+ expert interviews and stages partner workshops. We use AI agents over a structured rubric and ship in 5 days. Different cost structure — same buying job for businesses that don't need partner-level interviews.

Why more expensive than a Frost & Sullivan report?

That report sells to your competitor too. Ours profiles your 30 competitors, sizes your SAM, designs your three pricing tiers. Bespoke to you, not the category.

Will the AI hallucinate?

Every claim is sourced. Every multiplier in the market sizing has a citation or is explicitly flagged "estimated; to be validated." The operator reviews every artefact before delivery. The 100% rework guarantee covers the edge case.

Can I run it myself?

Yes. The template is open-source MIT-licensed. You're paying for the operator's editorial — knowing which 30 competitors to pick, where heatmap dimensions sit, what counts as a credible attack plan vs aspirational fluff.

What if my market is outside SG/HK?

Phase 1 is SG & HK because we know those markets cold (regulatory, grants, buyer behaviour). Phase 2 expands to MY/TH/PH/VN/ID at adjusted regional pricing. ID will be Q3 2026.

What if it's wrong?

Every fact is sourced — easy to challenge specific claims at the readout. We rework anything that doesn't hold up. If it still doesn't, full refund. The structured methodology means corrections are surgical.

What about confidentiality?

Standard NDA on request. We never sell or share your competitor list, your pricing analysis, or your strategy outputs. The methodology and template are open; your specific data is yours alone.

How do I share with my team?

The live admin dashboard ships with team-wide access for 12 months (Standard) or 24 months (Pro). Plus the PDF report — drop it in Slack, email, or your board pack.
