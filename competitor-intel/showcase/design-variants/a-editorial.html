§ I — The mid-market gap

Every alternative is wrong for businesses your size.

If you run a Singapore or Hong Kong business between USD 5M and 100M revenue, you've already tried — and rejected — every option below. None of them fit your budget and your need at the same time.

Big consulting

McKinsey, Bain, BCG

Won't take a project under USD 100k. A "diagnostic" runs USD 250k+. Out of band before the conversation starts.

USD 100k–3M+
Off-the-shelf

Frost & Sullivan, Statista

Affordable but generic. The same report sells to your competitor. Doesn't score your 30 firms against your buyer's evaluation.

USD 1.5–15k per report
CI software

Klue, Crayon, Kompyte

Tracks news and pricing. Doesn't size your TAM, recommend your tiers, or map your whitespace. Operations tool, not a deliverable.

USD 20–80k / yr
Freelance

Upwork, Toptal

Quality varies wildly. One person, six weeks, deck-driven. No reproducible methodology — if they leave, the work goes with them.

USD 2–16k

Eight artefacts. Bespoke to you. Five days to ship.
