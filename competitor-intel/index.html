Competitor Intel Template

Competitor Intel Template is a 9-agent competitive analysis pipeline that produces typed JSON handoffs for downstream construction toolkits. Built on the dt-site-creator stack, it sequences specialised agents — competitor research, market intelligence, pricing strategy, whitespace mapping, design auditing, visualisation, methodology curation, report generation, and aesthetics — into a repeatable workflow that turns raw landscape research into structured exhibits and decision-ready reports.

You are being redirected to the showcase exhibits. If your browser does not redirect automatically, the sections below summarise what the dashboard contains.

Competitor Atlas

A structured catalogue of competitors with normalised attributes — segment, positioning, pricing tier, geographic footprint, and observable signals — so patterns across the field become legible at a glance. See the showcase exhibits for live examples.

Pricing Intelligence

Comparative pricing analysis with tier breakdowns, anchor-point detection, and grade scoring across competitors. Output feeds directly into proposal pricing decisions and packaging recommendations.

Whitespace Map

Blue-ocean mapping that surfaces under-served segments, unmet jobs-to-be-done, and positioning gaps the existing field has missed. Each whitespace entry is typed JSON so downstream toolkits can act on it programmatically.

Methodology

Read the full methodology overview for agent-by-agent specifications, field dictionaries, and the JSON contract each stage emits. The template shows the empty shell that gets populated per engagement.
