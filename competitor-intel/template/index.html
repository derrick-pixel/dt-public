What ships in this fork

The boilerplate ships with five admin views (overview, insights, whitespace, report, design audit), one public landing (this page), and a typed JSON data layer under /data. The 9-agent pipeline produces data/*.json handoffs that drive every chart, table, and narrative line. Replace the sample data, swap the brand colours in assets/css/site.css, and you have a deployable competitive-intelligence dashboard for your own market.

  • Overview — KPI strip, top-5 threats, market sizing funnel
  • Insights — segment-level competitive observations and implications
  • Whitespace — segment×need heatmap and ranked attack plans
  • Report — printable board-grade synthesis
  • Design audit — top-15 sites scored on a 5-D rubric
