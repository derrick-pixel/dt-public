Market & Pricing Intelligence

This admin view fuses two outputs of the competitor-intel pipeline. The market intelligence half renders TAM/SAM/SOM from /data/market.json, the policy landscape (supportive vs adverse regulatory and grant signal), country-readiness scores across regulatory, tech maturity, and price tolerance, and the target-market distribution by category. The pricing strategy half renders persona ICPs with willingness-to-pay bands, recommended pricing tiers (with grant-effective post-PSG prices), and a competitor price comparison chart with the recommended tier line overlaid. Replace the JSON before shipping; the visualisations are pure functions of the data layer.
