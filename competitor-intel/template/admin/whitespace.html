Whitespace Atlas

Three artefacts work together to identify where the category is empty and where you can win. The strategy canvas radar shows where competitors cluster on the dimensions buyers evaluate, with your recommended position overlaid; flat patches in the radar are areas of similarity to attack with differentiation, sharp peaks are dimensions where everyone competes the same way. The segment×need heatmap counts how many competitors genuinely serve each pair (score ≥ 3) — green cells are open whitespace, amber is contested, red is crowded; click any cell to see who plays and how. The ranked attack plans are concrete go-to-market specs: an ICP, a TAM estimate with reasoning, a gap thesis, a why-we-win argument, and a GTM channel with pitch, pricing, and content idea.
